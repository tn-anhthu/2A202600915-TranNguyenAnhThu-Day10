④ Phase 2 — Corruption Applied

23 → 21 rows after corruption (seed = 42). Six types of corruption were applied to simulate real-world data quality degradation.
  • 1
    drop_latest 4 rows
    Dropped 4 newest records — reduces coverage and corpus recency.
  • 2
    blank_summary 3 rows
    Blanked abstract on 3 rows — summary_chars drops to 0, breaks embedding quality checks.
  • 3
    noise_injection 3 rows
    Prepended xΩ∅ [CORRUPTED_DATA] %# to 3 summaries — pollutes vector embeddings.
  • 4
    title_truncate 3 rows
    Truncated titles to 20 chars — breaks exact title lookup in the agent.
  • 5
    stale_date 2 rows
    Set published = 2019-01-01 on 2 rows — triggers freshness check failure.
  • 6
    add_duplicates 2 rows
    Added 2 duplicate paper_id rows — triggers paper_id_unique check failure.
